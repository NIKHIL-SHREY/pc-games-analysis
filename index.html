Why PC Games? Why This Dataset?

Gaming is a massive industry, influencing millions worldwide. This dataset was chosen to understand how game prices, ratings, and trends evolve over time.

We aim to answer key questions: What makes a game successful? Does price impact ratings? What genres dominate? Let’s find out!
